tic tac toe pseudocode
structure
    play area div in center of page
        9 divs arranged in grid with borders on edges that form tic tac toe board
        hidden X and O's behind each of the 9 divs that will activate when clicked

    title div centered above play area

    divs on right and left of play area that display player info
        player names,
        an indicator for whose turn it is, 
        and text of 'winner!' for whoever wins the game

    div under play area that displays 'tie' if there is a tie
    reset button div at bottom of page
